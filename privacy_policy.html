Foreman — Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2026

Foreman ("the app") is built for tradespeople who value their privacy. This policy explains what data we collect, why, and how it's stored.

Data we collect

  • Job data (titles, client names, addresses, notes, time entries, photos, invoice items) — entered by you, stored locally on your device and optionally synced to your private iCloud account.
  • Business settings (business name, phone, email, logo, hourly rate) — stored locally and in your private iCloud.
  • Purchase information — handled entirely by Apple's App Store and StoreKit. We never see or store payment details.

Data we do NOT collect

  • We do not collect analytics, crash reports, or usage telemetry.
  • We do not share any data with third parties.
  • We do not have servers. All your data lives on your device and in your private iCloud account (if you enable iCloud sync).

iCloud sync

If you are signed into iCloud, Foreman syncs your data to your private iCloud database using CloudKit. This data is accessible only to you — we have no access to it.

Photos and camera

Photos you attach to jobs are stored locally in the app's document directory and optionally synced via iCloud. They are never uploaded anywhere else.

Location

Foreman requests location access only when you tap "Use My Location" in the job form. Location data is stored as part of your job record and is never transmitted to us.

Children

Foreman is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

Changes to this policy

We may update this policy. Continued use of the app after changes means you accept the updated policy.
